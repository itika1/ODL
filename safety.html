Safety

Our Work is Safe and Customer Satisfactory

The Company is dedicated to maintaining a safe working environment under the Construction Regulations for all employees and sub-contractors. Significant resources are deployed to ensure best practice in the area of Health & Safety.

All out staff are up to date with the latest health and safety legislation and best practices in the construction industry.

Our staff are trained and certified to industry standards. Some of our certifications include: SOLAS Safe Pass, IOSH Managing Safely in Construction, Manual Handling, Abrasive Wheel, and Construction Skilled Certification Scheme(CSCS) for Site Dumpers, Mini Diggers and Excavators.

We are a building contractor based in Cork. We cover all aspects of construction work quoted for and undertaken.
