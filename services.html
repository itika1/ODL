Renovations

If you are looking to renovate your bathroom, kitchen or a whole home renovation, our team can transform your home to a place where you can relax and enjoy the space.

Attic Conversions

Our work has involved converting attics into a bedroom, study, and children's playroom. All insulation materials are fitted by our skilled installers ensuring minimum heat loss and financial savings on a annual basis.

New Build - PSCS

If you are at the start of your new build or deciding on the design/location, please feel free to contact us for a no-obligation quotation.

Extensions

From advising you on building regulations to taking care of the energy efficiency of the extension, we have specialist available to advise you. We can provide you with a free quotation that will include a summary of what we will cover in the extension and can also discuss with you what grants if any are available to you.

Ground Works

We have carried out groundworks including site clearance, foundations, floors, footpaths, drainage and sewage, landscaping, driveways, patios, and paving for residentail & commercial clients

Carpentry Works

We offer a complete suite of carpentry works from new roofs, roofing repairs, flooring, stairs, as well as first fix and second fix for residentail & commercial clients
We are a building contractor based in Cork. We cover all aspects of construction work quoted for and undertaken.
